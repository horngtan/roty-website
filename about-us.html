About Us

Our journey with ROTY began in 2022, driven by a personal need for something we just couldn’t find. My brother Jasbir and I, Harneet, found ourselves struggling to balance busy schedules with the desire for fresh, healthy, and comforting meals that didn’t involve the usual fast food or takeout.

Every day, we’d see so many friends, colleagues, and even family members dealing with the same dilemma—torn between eating out and sacrificing the quality of home-cooked meals.

We wanted a solution that wasn’t just convenient but actually aligned with what we missed from home: nutritious, freshly cooked food made from natural, quality ingredients. We dreamed of bringing the simplicity and authenticity of home-style meals to anyone who craved it, especially in today’s fast-paced world where cooking from scratch can feel out of reach.

That’s how ROTY was born—a way for us to bring the flavors and care of a home-cooked meal to people like us, who want convenience without compromising on health or taste.

Today, every meal we deliver is crafted with the same dedication and love we put into our own family meals, with freshly prepared curries and tiffins made from scratch each day. We hope that, through ROTY, you feel not just fed but truly cared for—like family.
